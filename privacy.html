Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Chikane ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our app. This policy applies to all users of the Chikane app and related services. Please review this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

Data Collection

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your username and email address.
  • Profile Information: You may choose to provide additional information such as your name and profile picture.
  • Vehicle Information: Details about your vehicles, including make, model, year, and optional performance data.
  • Session Data: Information about your track sessions, including lap times, telemetry data, and performance metrics.
  • Location Data: We collect GPS data during your track sessions to provide accurate tracking and mapping features. We also use location data to set start/finish lines for tracks.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the devices you use to access Chikane, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect data about how you interact with the app, including features used and time spent in the app.
  • Camera and Microphone Data: If you choose to record video during your sessions, we access your device's camera and microphone. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Data Usage

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Chikane app and its features.
  • To personalize your experience and improve our services.
  • To analyze performance data and generate insights about your track sessions.
  • To communicate with you about app updates, new features, and support requests.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent use of our services.
  • To generate aggregated, anonymous data for research and analysis to improve our services.

Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party services to help us operate our business and the Chikane app. These services may have access to your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase for authentication, data storage, and analytics. Your data is subject to Google's privacy policy when processed by Firebase.
  • WeatherKit: We use Apple's WeatherKit to provide weather information for your track sessions. Only your location data is shared with WeatherKit to fetch relevant weather data.
  • Analytics: We may use third-party analytics tools to help us measure traffic and usage trends for the app. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Your data is stored securely in Google Cloud Firestore, which provides robust security features.
  • All data transmissions between your device and our servers are encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • We regularly review our information collection, storage, and processing practices to guard against unauthorized access.
  • Access to personal information is restricted to authorized employees, contractors, and agents who need to know that information in order to operate, develop, or improve our services.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access and update most of your account information directly within the app.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app or by contacting us.
  • Opt-out: You can opt-out of non-essential data collection, such as anonymous usage data, through the app settings.
  • Push Notifications: You can manage your preferences for push notifications in the app settings or your device settings.

To exercise these rights or if you have any questions about your personal information, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
