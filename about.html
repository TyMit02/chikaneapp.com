From Racer to Creator

At 17 years old, I created Chikane to fill a gap in lap timing software. I started racing when I was 15 and quickly realized there wasn’t an app that combined precision, speed, and event management in one place. So, I built Chikane—a lap timing and event management software built by racers, for racers.
